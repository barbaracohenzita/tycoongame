How to Play

Goal: Be the first tycoon to reach $1,000,000 net worth, or the last one standing after 60 turns.

Turns: Each turn you can buy businesses, upgrade them, and research tech. Then hit END TURN — rivals move, markets shift, income pays out.

Factions: Your faction gives you +10% income on its home sector.

Market Phases: Boom (1.3x), Normal (1.0x), Recession (0.9x), Crash (0.7x). Sectors swing independently.

Strategy: Diversify across sectors to survive crashes. Upgrade top earners. Research tech for permanent bonuses.
